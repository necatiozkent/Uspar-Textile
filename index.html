Brief History

The origins of the USPAR TEKSTIL SANAYI VE TICARET A.S. dates back to 1970’s, during which its late founder, Mr. Ismail USLU ,father of Ahmet and Fevzi USLU, was trying to earn his own living as a yarn and raw fabric trader in Istanbul.​ 1985 Mr. USLU founded USLU TEKSTIL Inc.​1986 Mr.USLU kept on his attempts by opening the first Open-End plant in DENIZLI Organized Industrial Zone.​1994 Second Open-End plant was started manufacturing to keep up the changing market conditions. 1997 Ismail USLU and Enver PARALI families combined their strength with the name “USPAR Tekstil Inc.” and became partners.

Milestones
